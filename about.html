About Our Gurudwara

Welcome to Gurudwara Sri Guru Nanak Sat Sang Sabha, a sacred place of worship, community, and service. Established in 1985, our Gurudwara has been a spiritual home for thousands of devotees, fostering the teachings of Guru Nanak Dev Ji and the Sikh Gurus.

Our Gurudwara is not just a place of prayer but a center for education, charity, and unity. We follow the principles of Naam Japna (Meditation), Kirat Karni (Honest Living), and Vand Chakna (Sharing with Others).

Every day, we welcome people from all walks of life to experience the divine peace of Gurbani Kirtan, partake in Langar (free community kitchen), and engage in selfless service (Seva).

Our Mission

To spread the divine message of Guru Nanak Dev Ji by promoting spiritual growth, community service, and universal brotherhood. We strive to provide a welcoming space for worship, education, and selfless service.

Our Vision

To create a society where people live with truth, compassion, and equality, inspired by Sikh values. We aim to serve humanity through Langar, education, and interfaith harmony.
